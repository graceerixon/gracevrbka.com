01 The work currently in production
Flagship 2024 → now

Superb Shifts. A two-sided healthcare staffing platform I've been fortunate enough to help build and scale.

Workers onboard from their phone. Facilities post shifts. Compliance, payments, and communication handled end-to-end. I joined as a full-stack engineer, was promoted to CTO 18 months in, and have been the sole engineer since October 2025. I still write code every day, owning architecture, infrastructure, security, ongoing maintenance, and new feature development end-to-end.

A

Worker onboarding

Signup to shift-ready, all from a phone. Dynamic multi-step forms, document upload and in-browser PDF signing, license verification with multi-state tracking, emergency contacts, and tax consent. Built on React Hook Form and Azure Blob Storage.

B

Compliance engine

Regulatory rules for 52 state requirements encoded as data. Ready Health background checks, skills checklists, WV CARES, TB, influenza/MMR, and compliance briefcase PDFs for facility audits. 86,726 documents managed, 3,081 background checks processed, and 46,592 expiration reminders sent, all tracked and audited automatically.

C

Subcontractor portal

A new product line I designed and shipped end-to-end. 20+ agency-facing pages plus admin counterparts, a full NestJS backend module, state contract management, and dedicated RBAC. Third-party agencies onboard, manage workers, and track placements through the platform. Opened a new revenue channel for the business.

D

Realtime comms

Chat, video, push, SMS, and email through one pipeline, built on Stream, Firebase, Twilio, and SendGrid. The channel matches the urgency.
